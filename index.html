You’re one of the most beautiful women I’ve ever met—not just physically, but in the way you care and love, even for those you barely know. Thank you for stepping into my life with such grace and warmth. You have this effortless way of making heavy things feel light. You’ve shown me that real friendship isn’t loud or flashy—it’s steady. Love you always,
Your very lucky sister.
